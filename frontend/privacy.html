🔐 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to HostelBuddy. Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how HostelBuddy collects, uses, protects, and manages user information when you use our platform.

By accessing or using HostelBuddy, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

📌 1. Information We Collect

1.1 Student Information

When students create an account or use the platform, we may collect:

  • Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number
  • Profile Information & Preferred Hostel Location
  • Enquiry Messages sent to hostel owners

1.2 Hostel / PG Owner Information

To ensure trust and authenticity, we collect additional details from property owners:

  • Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address
  • Property Details, Coordinates, Amenities & Images
  • Identity Verification Documents (such as Aadhaar)

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

Technical information automatically collected helps improve performance:

  • IP Address, Device Type, Browser
  • Interaction data (clicks, searches, filters used)

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

HostelBuddy uses collected data only for legitimate platform purposes, including:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable searches and enquire between students/owners
  • Verify property owners and maintain platform trust
  • Prevent fraud or misuse of the platform

We never sell personal user data to third parties.

🔄 3. Information Sharing

Student → Owner: When you enquire, the owner sees your Name, Contact Info, and Message.

Owner → Students: Students view property details, images, and pricing. Contact info is shared only after enquiry approval.

🛡️ 4. Data Security

We implement multiple safeguards:

  • Secure cloud storage for images via Cloudinary
  • Encrypted data handling practices
  • Controlled admin access to sensitive information
  • Verified property approval system

✔️ 5. Verification Process

Owners may be required to submit identity documents. These are accessed only by the Admin team and are NOT visible to students or other users. This helps prevent scams.

❌ 6. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion at any time. Upon deletion, personal profile data will be removed from the platform.

📞 7. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact our team at:

Email: support@hostelbuddy.com

⚖️ Agreement

By using HostelBuddy, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
