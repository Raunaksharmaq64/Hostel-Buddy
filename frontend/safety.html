🛡️ Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. HostelBuddy is designed for security, but collective awareness is key.

🎓 Safety Guidelines for Students

Finding a hostel is a big decision — don’t rush it.

🔍 1. Verify Before Finalizing

  • Always visit the property physically before booking.
  • Check: Room condition 🛏️, Washroom hygiene 🚿, Food quality 🍽️.
  • Do not rely only on photos; physical inspection is mandatory.

📞 2. Communicate Carefully

  • Use HostelBuddy’s enquiry system first to keep communication records.
  • Avoid sharing personal details too early.
  • Be cautious if an owner pressures you to book quickly or refuses a visit.

💰 3. Avoid Advance Payments Without Proof

  • Never send money without visiting the property and receiving proper confirmation.
  • Always ask for a Receipt and a Written Agreement.

🧾 4. Check Owner Verification

Prefer listings with the “Verified Badge” ✔️. Verified owners are manually reviewed by our Admin team.

🚨 Report Suspicious Activity: Immediately report fake photos, misleading details, unsafe behavior, fraud, or payment scams via support.

🏠 Safety Guidelines for Hostel Owners

Maintaining platform trust is a shared responsibility.

✔️ 1. Provide Genuine Information

  • Upload real, current images of the actual rooms and facilities.
  • Mention correct pricing, available facilities, and house rules.

🤝 2. Maintain Professional Communication

  • Respond politely and promptly to student enquiries.
  • Clearly explain Rent, Food charges, and Security deposit terms.

🧾 3. Verify Tenants Properly

  • Collect valid ID proof from students during the onboarding process.
  • Maintain basic records for safety and local compliance.

🔒 4. Ensure Property Safety

  • Provide secure locks 🔐 and a hygienic environment 🧹.
  • Implement basic safety measures throughout the premises.

🚫 Violation of these terms may result in immediate account suspension.

🛡️ Platform Safety Measures

HostelBuddy actively works to keep the platform secure:

  • Verified Listings: Only admin-approved properties go live.
  • Owner Verification: Identity checks reduce fake listings.
  • Monitoring System: Admin tracks and flags suspicious activities.
  • Controlled Enquiry: Communication begins within the secure platform.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

HostelBuddy acts as a platform to connect students and owners. We do NOT physically verify every property daily, handle payments, or guarantee user behavior. Final decisions must be made carefully by the users.
