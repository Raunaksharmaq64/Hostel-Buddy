Welcome to HostelBuddy

Chahe aap student ho ya hostel owner — yeh platform dono ke liye useful hai. Is guide me hum step-by-step batayenge ki platform ko kaise use karna hai.

👨‍🎓 For Students

Kaise Find Karein Apna Perfect Room?

1

Search Hostels

Home page par jaake search bar me apni location enter karein (e.g., "Hostels near LNCT Bhopal"). Aap price range, food, aur room type ke basis par filter bhi kar sakte hain.

2

View Detailed Insights

Kisi bhi hostel par click karke aap uski HQ photos, mess menu, room interiors, aur pricing details dekh sakte hain. Sab kuch transparent hai!

3

Send Enquiry & Start Chat

Interest ho toh "Send Enquiry" click karein. Aap directly owner se chat karke availability ya koi bhi specific sawal puch sakte hain.

4

Schedule Visit

Owner aapko reply karega aur aap visit schedule kar sakte hain. Aapki security ke liye platform ensure karta hai ki owners verified hon.

5

Manage Your Profile

Apni profile me contact details aur documents add karein taaki owners aapko trust kar sakein aur booking jaldi ho jaye.

🏠 For Hostel / PG Owners

Apna Business Kaise Grow Karein?

1

Register as Owner

Account create karte waqt "Owner" role choose karein. Aapko apni identity verify karwani hogi premium experience dene ke liye.

2

Add Multiple Properties

Apna dashboard use karke ek ya ek se zyada hostel add karein. Name, address, aur price accurate rakhein.

3

High-Quality Photos

Room, Mess, aur Washroom ki clean photos upload karein. Jitni achi photos, utne zyada conversion!

4

Real-time Enquiries

Students jab enquiry bhejenge toh aapke dashboard me turant dikhegi. Respond karein aur booking confirm karein.
